Frequently Asked Questions

Lost & Found is a platform that helps users report lost items and allows finders to upload items they have found. The platform uses AI to match lost and found items, making it easier to reunite owners with their belongings.

You can report a lost item by signing up on our website, filling out a simple form with details of the lost item, and uploading a photo if possible. Our AI will help you find matches with items reported as found.

If you find an item, simply create an account, report the found item, and upload photos. The platform will match the item with potential owners, making it easy to return the lost item.

If your lost item remains unclaimed for 6 months, it will be donated to verified charities, orphanages, or NGOs.
